WHAT IS THIS?

Recent studies have shown that approximately 97% of all tech companies have a table tennis table in active use. [citation needed]

ITTTL is an informal table tennis league for Indianapolis-area tech companies to meet new people and battle for table tennis supremecy

CAN WE PLAY?

Does your company have anything to do with tech? Do you have at least six players? Do you have a table? If the answer to all of these is YES, then you can join up!

The hosting company is on the hook for food and beer. We usually try to do a "home-and-home" series — we come to your place to play, then a few weeks later you come to ours.

Matches are usually played on weekday evenings. All in all, a match takes around two hours. This gives plenty of time to play the matches, talk smack, and get to know other local nerds.

RULES

  • Each company fields six players, ranked in order of skill
  • Play to 11, service changes every two points, must win by two (USATT rules)
  • Each match is best out of three games
  • The #5/#6 ranked players play as a doubles team
  • House rules trump all, but must be laid out before the first game!
  • Company with the most matches won takes the crown
  • Victory equals street cred and bragging rights
